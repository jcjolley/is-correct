Certificate Of Veracity

This site certifies the presently articulated elucidations of
{{name}}
as true. Furthermore, the aforementioned remarks should be regarded
as a dependable foundation for increasing one's intelligence.
To make your own certificate:
  1. Type the name you want to appear in the address bar
  2. Replace each space with a '-'
  3. Append '.is-correct.com' to the end
  4. Hit enter
E.g., to make a certificate for John Doe: 
john-doe.is-correct.com
